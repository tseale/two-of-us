# Two of Us — Privacy Policy

*Effective July 13, 2026*

Two of Us is a private baby tracker for parents: feeds, sleep, and diaper
changes, shared between the people you invite and no one else. This policy
explains what the app stores, where it lives, and who can see it. The short
version: **your data stays in your own iCloud, and we never see it.**

## What the app stores

- Your baby's name, date of birth, and an optional photo you choose.
- The care events you log: feeds (amount and time), sleep (start and end),
  diaper changes (type and time), and any notes you add — along with which
  parent or caregiver logged each one.
- The name, color, role, and optional photo of each person you invite.
- Shared feeding preferences (like your target feed interval).

Notification preferences and quiet hours stay on your own device and are never
synced anywhere.

## Where your data lives

Everything above is stored in **your own private iCloud**, using Apple's
CloudKit. There is **no Two of Us server**. We (the developer) have no way to
read, receive, or access your data — it moves between your devices and the
people you invite entirely inside Apple's iCloud.

Because of this, the App Store privacy label for Two of Us is **"Data Not
Collected"**: no data of any kind is collected by the developer or by third
parties.

## Who can see your data

Only you and the people you explicitly invite by link. There are no public
profiles, no discovery, and no view-only access for anyone else. You can remove
a person's access at any time from Settings → People; removal takes effect
immediately.

## No tracking, no ads, no third parties

- No analytics, telemetry, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
- No third-party SDKs — Two of Us is built only with Apple's own frameworks.
- Notifications are generated locally on your device. Nothing about your baby
  is routed through any service other than Apple's iCloud.

## Photos

If you add a photo for your baby or a caregiver, it's chosen through the
system photo picker — the app never gets access to your photo library. Photos
are downscaled on your device and stored with the rest of your data in your
iCloud.

## Your control

- **Export:** you can export your full log as a CSV file or a printable PDF
  report at any time, generated on your device. It goes only where you choose
  to share it.
- **Delete:** Settings → Manage data → Delete everything permanently removes
  all data from iCloud for every member of the household. Deleting the app
  alone does not delete your iCloud data (so a reinstall can restore it).

## Children's privacy

Two of Us is used by parents and caregivers to record the care *they* provide.
It is not directed at children, has no child-facing accounts or features, and
collects no data from children (or from anyone else — see above).

## Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app changes any of the above — for example, by
adding an optional feature that transmits data anywhere other than your iCloud
— this policy will be updated first, and the App Store privacy label with it.

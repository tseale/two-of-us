Privacy

The short version: your data stays in your own iCloud, and we never see it. The app's App Store privacy label is Data Not Collected.

All data lives in your iCloud
Baby name and date of birth, feed amounts, sleep times, diaper logs, and participant names are stored in your private iCloud via CloudKit. There is no Two of Us server.
We can't see your data
Data flows between your devices and the people you invite entirely inside Apple's iCloud. The developer has no access.
No tracking, ads, or analytics
No analytics SDKs, no advertising, no telemetry. The app uses only Apple's own frameworks — nothing from any third party.
Invite-only access
Only the people you explicitly invite by link can see your data. Access is revocable any time from Settings → People.
You're in control
Export your full log as a CSV or PDF at any time, generated on-device. Delete everything via Settings → Manage data — permanently removes all data from iCloud.

About

The Two of Us is a native iOS app built for parents who want a fast, calm way to track newborn care together. No accounts, no subscriptions, no cloud service of our own — just your iPhone and your iCloud.

Built with SwiftUI, SwiftData, and CloudKit. Available on TestFlight.
